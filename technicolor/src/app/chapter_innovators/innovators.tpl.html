40,000 A-HA'S AND COUNTING

Since 1892, Technicolor and Thomson (our heritage company) have been delivering constantly innovative and reliable technologies for both industrial and consumer use. We rank among the worldwide leaders in patent licensing for audio and video technologies, with more than 40,000 patents and applications from more than 5,600 inventions. 80% of today's consumer electronics manufacturers integrate our intellectual property.

FOCUSED ON CONSUMERS AND THE FUTURE

At research and innovation centers around the globe, our engineers and technicians continue to build our extraordinary portfolio of advanced technologies and solutions. The Technicolor approach to technology licensing is built upon partnership. Rather than simply provide use of a patent, we work side-by-side with manufacturers to successfully implement the technology in whatever form it takes.

Immersive media

Research and innovation in this area focuses on two key areas that continue to make media and content more immersive:
