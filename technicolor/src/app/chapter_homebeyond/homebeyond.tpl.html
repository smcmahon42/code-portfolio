Innovation comes home

As entertainment at home continues to evolve beyond physical media, we’re active in groundbreaking digital delivery. Our next-generation entertainment app, M-Go, makes it easy to watch the movies and TV shows you love at home or on all your favorite mobile devices. M-Go offers seamless access to movies, music, apps, live TV and more – all through your cloud-connected devices. This advanced app is available as a free download on all major operating systems and is preloaded on select Samsung and Vizio smart TVs and blu-ray players, as well as Intel Ultrabooks. M-Go is a joint venture of Technicolor and DreamWorks Animation and is also partnered with Hollywood’s major studios.
